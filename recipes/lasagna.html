Lasagna

Description

A vegan Lasagne Dish, easily made

  • 200g Tofu or vegan Beef
  • 500g of chopped or strained Tomatoes
  • 150g of vegan Cheese
  • 250g of Lasagna Pasta Plates
  • 1 Handfull of fresh Basil
  • 1-2 Onions
  • A Tablespoon of Olive Oil
  • A Tablespoon of Flou
  • 250ml Plant Milk unsweetened
  • A Tablespoon of Margarine

Steps

  1. Cut the Onions in little cubes and crumble the Tofu
  2. Give the Olive Oil in a pan and set it to middle heat
  3. Put the sliced onion in the pan and roast them until they are transparent
  4. Add the Tofu to the Pan
  5. Add the whole can of tomatoes
  6. Chop the Basil and add it to the pan
  7. Season to taste with salt and pepper
  8. Set it to a low heat and let it rest
  9. Take a second pan or pot and melt the Margarine it
  10. Add the Flour to it and stir it well while adding
  11. Let it rest for a Minute then add the Milk while stirring
  12. Season it
  13. Let it stay on low heat until it bubbles start to rise, stir it again
  14. Use a tray to plate the Pastasauce and Bechamelsauce with the Noodle Plates
  15. Bake it at 200Â°C for 35-40 Minutes
